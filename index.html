What is the Black Lives Matter Movement?

The Black Lives Matter Movement is an international movement that campaigns against systematic racism and violence that is being inflicted on the African American community. People around the world have been fighting for the Black Lives Matter Movement by signing petitions, protesting, and donating money. Many people have also been posting about this movement to help spread awareness and educate others about what is going on. By speaking up and supporting this movement, you can make a huge impact!



Why is it Important That We Educate Ourselves During This Time?

The more you educate yourself, the more likely you are to participate in this movement. Since this movement requires everyone's support, it is important that everyone takes the time to educate themselves and participate. Turning away from what is happening in the world right now will not make an impact. Additionally, it is important to remember to have uncomfortable conversations about what is happening with family members and friends.

Why Black Lives Matter?

You may be asking: Why Black Lives Matter? Don't all lives matter? The simple answer: yes, all lives do matter. But the Black people of our community are being marginalized and misrepresented.They are experiencing not only injustice but brutality by our criminal justice system.
